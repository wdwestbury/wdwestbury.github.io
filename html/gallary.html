Capstone Project

Interactive 2016 Elections Map

Prior to the 2016 general election, Wisconsin Public Radio requested a revision of their online election map. After reviewing other media outlets and researching online mapping technologies I created a new and improved election mapping application that could be embedded in their website and used in the 2016 general election and beyond. The new application displays data through the use of scalable vector graphics and is designed to have a clear user interface and good user experience across a variety of devices. The images above were taken durning the 2016 general election and show how the map looks on desktop and mobile devices.
